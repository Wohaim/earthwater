OUR TECHS

The First Approach:

Optimising Water Linked Systems with High-Throughput Data and Machine Learning

It is crucial to understand the level of surface water runoff in order to balance and optimize downstream reliant usages.

However, a lack of near real data during precipitation and runoff, limits our understanding and ability to optimizes linked water systems, such as hydropower plants that support the grid, water treatment facilities that treat storm water, flood prevention and prediction, and much more. 

Earth Water generates and analyses big data needed to to discover and optimize downstream usage of surface runoff during precipitation events, using machine-learning models.

The Second Approach:

Modular, Decentralized, Nano-Technology for Increased Access to Clean Water

Treating water in a decentralized structure, not only reduce the burden on current treatment facilities and technologies but also reduce the turnaround time for clean fresh water.

Conversely, downstream wastewater treatment has to go through several stages, requiring a large amount of energy and chemicals, slowing down the rate at which clean water becomes available. Our decentralized modular infrastructure, working at the nano scale, is a single stage treatment facilitator.

Our Technologies:

Measuring and Treating Water at Large Scale

Hydro-Lynk is the leading sensor fusion platform for quantitative characterization of surface water runoff that feed to downstream Hydro systems. Leveraging artificial intelligence, we are aiming to generate the largest repository of machine learning models, that we can train, capable of predicting and forecasting, exceedance, using low-cost lidar fusion sensor technology. Computational designs are measured experimentally for model validation and refinement through rapid iterations.

Hydro-Cap togather with NASA, have invented a unique water sieving technology, that makes any contaminated water, clean and safe. The contaminated water is separated using a combination of selected nano-vibrations (acoustic paired) and carbon nanotubes (CNT’s) across a molecular sieve membrane technology. Turning the idea of filtration on its head, this technology selectively targets water molecules only, by pushing water away from contaminants, rather than removing contaminants from water, as you would find in a reverse osmosis system.
