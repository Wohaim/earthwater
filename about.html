About

Earth Water leverages machine learning and nano technology to measure and predict water interactions for water linked systems and treat contaminated water.

Our vision is to improve access to clean water while using water as a means to optimise other downstream water dependent fundamentals such as hydropower plants. Our HydroWink platforms generate and analyse the big data needed to discover and optimize downstream usage of surface runoff during precipitation events, using machine-learning models. Our HydroLynk platform uses an advance modular, decentralize, nano technology and acoustic vibration to treat contaminated water.
